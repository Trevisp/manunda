About Us

Kenyas best matatu.Bringing the streets to life..

Opposite Aka Manunda is a matatu known for its unique design inspired by Canada. This iconic vehicle has won the Ma3 Gala awards three times in a row. Operating on the Umoja route, it has become a popular and recognizable sight all over Nairobi.


Our History

Opposite was founded in 2019 by that time it was known as Dudu Mbaya.It managed to win the Gala Matatu awards as the best matatu in kenya. From that time it has been one of the most popular matatus in Nairobi for its unique design and amazing crew.The matatu has continued to be the best matatu in Nairobi as it has won the ma3 gala awards 3 times in row making history as the only matatu to do that.Opposite still continues to be one of the Nairobi finest despite facing stiff competition from other matatus like Matrix,Money fest,Detox e.t.c.
